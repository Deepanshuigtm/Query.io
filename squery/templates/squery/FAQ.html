How to solve your small problems directly without having to go to mentor ?

You can ask all your small queries like whome you have to contact and where you have to go to fix our small issues without directly going to the mentor by writing your issues in the CHATBOT. For example: whome we have to contact for mess related issues. So the CHATBOT will provide you the contact no. of the authenticated person whome you should contact to solve your issue.
